Are you passionate about new technology and looking forward to work on next generation GPU architecture? Do you love to provide innovative solutions and have fun while working on complex problems? Guide and drive the complete Product life cycle from design, to development and improvement of GPU System software.

Bachelors or Master’s degree in Computer Engineering/ Science
8+ years of industry and relevant experience.
Excellent C programming skills

Exposure to/ experience in Display technology and protocols like DisplayPort, HDMI, HDR
